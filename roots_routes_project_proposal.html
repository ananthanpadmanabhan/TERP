Roots & Routes

A New Vision for Responsible Tourism in Kerala

The Challenge: Bridging the Gap in Tourism

While Kerala's tourism thrives, its benefits often miss the grassroots entrepreneurs and communities that form the cultural backbone of the state. Mainstream circuits frequently overlook the small-scale, authentic experiences that travellers increasingly seek.

This chart illustrates a common scenario where the majority of tourism revenue is captured by larger commercial entities, leaving a smaller portion for local SMEs. "Roots & Routes" aims to shift this balance.

The Solution: A Digital Matchmaking Platform

"Roots & Routes" is a digital platform designed to connect travellers directly with verified, underrepresented local businesses. It transforms tourism into a force for equitable growth, cultural preservation, and authentic experiences.

Projected Target Impact

"Roots & Routes" is designed to deliver measurable, positive outcomes for all stakeholders, strengthening Kerala's entire responsible tourism ecosystem.
